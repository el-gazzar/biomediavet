Vit-C® 50% Powder

Composition :


Each 1Kg contains :


Ascorbic acid (vitamin C) 500 gm.
Sodium bicarbonate 50 gm.
Dextrose up to 1 kg.

Indications:


- Vit-C® powder is indicated for Vitamin C deficiency especially in fish and cases of stress, gastritis in horses, supportive treatment in coccidiosis in poultry, animal wound healing and healing of ulcers, burn, fracture, prevention of gout, development of animal immunity system etc. - It is especially valuable in tuberculosis of lungs, respiratory distress, bronchitis etc.
- It also acts as an antioxidant and prevents ageing.

Dosage & Administration:


Fish:

1 kg vitamin C-50% for 1 ton feed during processing.

Poultry:

1 gm / litre of drinking water daily for 3-4 consecutive days.

Large animal:

1 gm vit-C® liquid per 10 kg body weight daily for 3-4 consecutive days.

Preparation:

500 gm & 1 Kg.

Withdrawal times:

none.

Storage:

Stored away of heat and direct sunlight and humidity.

M.O.A. Reg . No: 933 /2015.

When administered subcutaneously massage the injection site gently .
