CALCIMIX®
(Calcium, Magnesium And Vitamins Supplement)
500 ml Sterile liquid Product

Composition :


Each 100 ml contains :


Calcium gluconate: 25 gm.
Magnesium chloride: 5 gm.
CPhosphorus (Beutaphosphan): 20 gm.
Vitamin B1 (Thiamine): 20 mg.
Vitamin B2 (Riboflavin): 8 mg.
Vitamin B5 (Panthenol): 10 mg.
Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine): 20 mg.
D-Glucose: 6 gm.
Dist. Water up to 100 ml.

Indications:


- Calcimix® product is recommended for treatment of hypocalcaemia in cattle and sheep when complicated by magnesium and phosphorus deficiency. - For treatment of Hypocalcaemia before, during and after birth, or during lactation.
- Calcimix® used in cases of Downer cow syndrome (including during pregnancy) caused by deficiency diseases or internal secretory dysfunction. - Use in conjunction with vitamin B complex to prevent anemia, enhance metabolism and prevent diarrhea in cow and buffalo.

Dosage:


- Administer by subcutaneous or slow intravenous injection.
- As a guide it is suggested that a dose of 100 ml / 500 kg B.W is given slowly intravenously, followed by 100 ml / 500 kg B.W subcutaneously.

Precautions :


Administer with care particularly where concurrent toxaemia exists.
The solution should be warmed to body temperature before use.
Intravenous injections should be carried out slowly and stopped at once if untoward symptoms occur.
Rapid intravenous injection may result in cardiac arrhythmias and in severely toxaemic cows, collapse and death. When administered subcutaneously massage the injection site gently .

Packaging :

500 ml clear plastic vial.

Storage:

Store at temperatures below 30°C , avoid freezing.

Expiry date:

24 months of production date.

M.O.A. Reg . No: 4117 /2016.
