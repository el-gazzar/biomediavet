Welcome To Bio Media
BioMedia® Company and factory established in 2010, Biomedia® factory is committed to providing market-driven & high quality animal health and animal feed products vitamins premixes and enzymes throughout the all Egypt Governorates.
BioMedia offers innovative solutions in areas of animal nutrition and animal feeding additives that enable a more natural approach to animal production.
We manufacture, trade and export animal nutrition products, dietetics and natural animal feeding additives under the ISO 9001:2000 norm.
We can offer a wide range of products such as mycotoxins binders and detoxifiers, nutraceuticals, phytobiotics, natural growth promoters, acidifiers, binders, antioxidants, mould inhibitors, Salmonella control products, improvers of bed quality, electrolytes,immunostimulants, mineral and vitamin correctors, milk replacers, prebiotics and probiotics for poultry, ruminants, pets and equines.
We also have a very specific line of natural aquaculture additive products. Biomedia® continually monitors the industry for new products and new product ideas.
The company’s development program is based on analyzing the market and providing those products that meet the needs of end-users.
The Biomedia® Co., and factory product line has evolved from four main sources: Company research and development; reformulating and repackaging established products to add value; reintroducing popular, but often hard-to-find existing products; and registering Egyptian versions of proven international products.
Our factory is a strong believer that the Veterinary Pharmaceutical industry requires a self-regulatory body which is modelled on tried and tested systems.
It is of the utmost importance that this industry takes a responsible approach for Health and Safety of the individual and the Environment and works in close co-operation and synergy with the relevant regulatory bodies.
The company is at the forefront of formulation technology and has developed many new chemical routes for the synthesis of active materials. Biomedia has formulated and registered more than 40 products in Egypt.
Our Mission
It is our mission to fulfill the requirements and expectations of our stakeholders (customers, employees, shareholder and suppliers) preconceiving profitability and sustainability considering best possible fulfillment of the requirements in quality, environment, health and safety as well as security.
